About Us

LoveGarden Sounds has been a staple of the Lawrence, KS music scene for decades, providing a unique haven for music lovers and vinyl collectors alike. With a carefully curated selection of records, our store brings together a community of artists, audiophiles, and casual listeners. Whether you're seeking rare finds, indie hits, or timeless classics, LoveGarden has something for everyone.

Come explore, discover new sounds, and join us in celebrating the magic of music.
